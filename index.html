What is Map that Splat?

Map that Splat is an Android mapping app revolving around mapping roadkill. In MapThatSplat, the user can upload their own Roadkill, with an image, the whereabouts of the Roadkill (via GPS, or just by using the map to pin-point the location). One can also view a graph of the world
